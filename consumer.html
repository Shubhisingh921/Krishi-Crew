Our Features

Supporting Local Farmers

One of the key objectives of Krishi-Shop is to create a sustainable income for local farmers. By buying directly from farmers, the shop ensures that growers receive fair prices for their products, allowing them to reinvest in their farms and communities

Fruitful Delights

Krishi-Shop takes pride in their fruit section, which features a colorful display fruits from local orchards. Whether it's plump peaches in the summer, crisp apples in the fall, or sweet berries in the spring, the shop's fruit selection is ever-changing and always delightful, you can indulge in the pure taste of nature's bounty

Environmental Responsibility

Krishi-Shop implements eco-friendly practices throughout its operations. They encourage customers to bring their own containers, use compostable bags, and prioritize locally-sourced products to minimize transportation emissions.
